Yoga Breathing
or Pranayama

Yoga breathing, or pranayama, is a fundamental aspect of yoga practice that
involves the regulation of breath through various techniques. Pranayama is
derived from two Sanskrit words: "prana," meaning life force or vital energy,
and "ayama," meaning control or extension. Practicing pranayama can
have numerous benefits for physical, mental, and emotional health.
